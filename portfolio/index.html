About me
Who am I?

I'm Jescintha Arul, a passionate Computer Science & Engineering student at Geethanjali Institute of Science and Technology. I love turning ideas into code — whether that's a data structure puzzle on a whiteboard or a live web project deployed on GitHub.

My latest project, a horticulture website built during a community program, taught me just as much about understanding users as it did about plants. It reminded me that good software always starts with real people and real problems.

When I'm not writing Java or debugging SQL, I'm in the garden, at hack nights swapping ideas with teammates, or chasing new certifications to broaden my toolkit.

🌱 Goal: Craft fast, friendly systems that help real people — and dive deeper into machine learning to spot patterns humans miss.
